About

The Bear Sandwich Shop is a beloved local eatery, proudly founded and owned by Carmen Berzatto. Nestled in the heart of our community, it emerged in the place where Carmen's brother once ran the iconic The Original Beef of Chicagoland. With a passion for crafting delicious sandwiches and a commitment to local flavors, Carmen brings a fresh and innovative approach to our neighborhood. The Bear Sandwich Shop invites you to indulge in a delightful culinary journey, where every bite tells a story of family, tradition, and the love of great food.

Carmen Anthony "Carmy" Berzatto (Right), a Chicago native, is a global culinary sensation, achieving acclaim at 21 for his rare talent and dedication. From Paris to New York, he earned three Michelin stars. Now leading his family's restaurant, with his brothers best friend Richard "Richie" Jerimovich (left), Carmy weaves his expertise into a narrative shaped by the bittersweet legacy of his charismatic older brother, Mikey, and the enduring connection between flavors, family, and culinary artistry.

In memory of Michael "Mikey" Berzatto.
